Book Notes

I read a lot of nonfiction, and take detailed notes for most books I read. I think of these as sparknotes for books you give a shit about. Some things to know:

  1. If it's listed here, I think it's worth reading.
  2. They're categorized, generally with the books I recommend the most at the top.
  3. There aren't notes uploaded for all of them. If you see a book you'd like notes for shoot me a tweet or an email.

You can see a far more complete list of books I've read, including those I'm currently reading, here. If you want to talk books feel free to reach out!
